Drivers:
https://github.com/digistump/DigistumpArduino/releases
Digistump.Drivers.zip > DPinst64.exe

Board:
1) ArduinoIDE > File > Preferences
2) add URL to "Additional Boards Managers URLs":
http://digistump.com/package_digistump_index.json
3) Tools > Board: "Digispark (Default 16.5mhz)"
4) Programmer: "Micronucleus"
5) Hit Upload and then connect board
